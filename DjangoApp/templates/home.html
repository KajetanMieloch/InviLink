InviLink

InviLink is a web3 platform that allows event organizers to create and sell NFT-based tickets securely on the Solana blockchain. Attendees can purchase, trade, and verify their tickets seamlessly, ensuring authenticity and eliminating fraud. With full transparency, decentralized transactions, and a secondary marketplace, InviLink revolutionizes event access.
